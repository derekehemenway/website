Experience the power of rhythm!

Provide your group with a unique activity that promotes harmony, leadership, conflict resolution, and a cooperative spirit! Drumming can relieve stress, improve health, and well-being, promoto diversity and multicultural awareness.

Interactive drumming events are a blast for all participants! We offer events educational, organization, health and wellness, and community settings.

We hold weekly classes in which participants are introduced to a variety of world percussion traditions. Book us also for West African and Latin based live performances!

Available for individuals or groups, these sessions can help you with stress and personal issues. We will work with you to develop a customized therapeutic drumming program.

We sell and repair a variety of instruments including djembes, dunun (bass) drums, and conga drums. We also offer drum-making, maintenance, repair workshops.
