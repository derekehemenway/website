World Percussion Training and Performances

Ajana Drumming staff are trained in traditional West African and Latin rhythm performance and instruction. We hold regular classes in which participants are introduced to these world percussion traditions, and individual lessons are also available. Ajana Drumming has performed locally and nationally in formal performance and as well as at festival and community settings.
